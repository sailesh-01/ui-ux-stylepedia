About UI/UX Stylepedia

Welcome to the ultimate interactive compendium of UI/UX design styles! This project bridges the gap between static design inspiration and real, tactile frontend execution.

Why this exists

Design trends like Glassmorphism, Neumorphism, and Brutalism look amazing on Dribbble or static mockups, but understanding how they feel in practice—how buttons press, how sliders react, and how depth operates—requires live code.

The 4 Major Categories

  • Classic Foundations: The bedrock systems like Flat, Skeuomorphic, Material, Minimalist, and Metro.
  • Modern & Popular: Leading trends like Glassmorphism, Neumorphism, Claymorphism, Dark Mode, Apple HIG, and Aurora/Cosmic.
  • Experimental & Artistic: Expressive brands like Brutalism, Cyberpunk, Swiss Editorial, and Organic Biomorphic.
  • Platform & Industry: Tailored environments like Retro Terminal, Pixel Art, Analytics Dashboards, and E-commerce.

Crafted with pure HTML5, Vanilla CSS, and JavaScript. Zero external bloat, maximum visual fidelity.
